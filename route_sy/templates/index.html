基于Encoder-Decoder框架的图像描述生成研究

简介

给图像加文字描述，涉及在给定的图像（例如照片）的情况下生成人类可读的文本描述。 对于人类而言，这是一个容易解决的问题，但对于机器而言却非常具有挑战性，因为它既需要理解图像的内容，又需要将这种理解转化为自然语言。 本课题的任务是基于Encoder-Decoder框架，针对VGGNet识别到的图像中的对象，通过LSTM建立图像中对象之间的语义关系，加强对图像内容的深入理解，解决图像生成文本描述的难点并将计算机视觉和自然语言处理领域突破性地结合起来。

本模型根据图像对其生成描述，以下是一些样例：

"a group of people are watching a soccer game."

"a man is bowling ball by a bowling alley."

"a group of people are standing in front of a building."

"a man in a blue shirt is walking down a path."

"a group of people are walking down the street."

"a man is sitting on a chair in the street."

"a black dog with a stick in its mouth swims through the water."

"a man in a blue shirt is working on a machine."

"a young girl in a pink shirt is sitting on a chair."

"a skier is skiing down a snow covered mountain."

"a black and white dog with a red and white dog with a white dog."

"a man in a white shirt is cooking food."

"a band plays on stage."

"a brown dog is running through the water."

"a red and white car is driving down the road."

"a group of people are sitting on a raft in the water."
